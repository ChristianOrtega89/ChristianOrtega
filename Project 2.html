Rental Properties Dashboard

The Dashboard

Description

I created a simple and easy-to-use dashboard for quickly finding information about houses sold in a city. The dashboard breaks down simple insights like the average house price in different areas, the expected rent, and the total houses sold in each area. To gather this information, I web-scraped data from Zillow, and integrated it into Tableau using a Microsoft Excel document.

The dashboard gives real estate investors a quick look at which areas might be good for investment. It shows average prices and how many houses were sold, and it is interactive. Just click on a map area, and the info updates. There are also filters to narrow down results based on things like average price and monthly rent.

I made this dashboard because I'm passionate about real estate and have a future goal to become a successful real estate investor. I'm interested in in being able to access and use large amounts of house data to get an idea of an area's potential profitability for "buy and hold" properties. It is a tool to demonstrate my ability to source and visualize data and extract meaningful but simple insights in an easily digestible way.
